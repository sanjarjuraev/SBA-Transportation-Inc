Privacy Policy

At SBA Transportation Inc, we prioritize the security and confidentiality of your personal data. This privacy policy details our practices regarding the collection, usage, and protection of your information.

Information We Collect:

We may collect personal information such as your name, address, email address, and phone number. Additionally, we gather details about your shipments, including the weight, dimensions, destination address, and contents of your packages.

How We Use Your Information:

We utilize your personal information to process your shipments, communicate with you regarding your shipments, and provide customer support. Your information may also be used to enhance our services and to send you marketing communications, if you choose to receive them.

How We Protect Your Information:

We implement robust security measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. We employ encryption technologies and restrict access to personal information to authorized personnel only.

Your Choices:

You have the option to opt out of receiving marketing communications from us. You can also request access to, or deletion of, your personal information by reaching out to us.

Terms and Conditions:

These terms and conditions govern your use of SBA Transportation Inc's services. By utilizing our services, you consent to these terms and conditions.

Confidentiality:

We uphold the confidentiality of your information and will not disclose details about your shipments or business without your permission, except as necessitated by law or for facilitating the shipment process.

Shipment Acceptance:

We reserve the right to refuse shipments that we determine to be unsafe, illegal, or in violation of our policies. We may also inspect and open shipments as required by law.

Shipment Delivery:

We strive to deliver your shipments as per our stated delivery schedules, however, we do not guarantee specific delivery times and are not responsible for delays or damages caused by circumstances beyond our control, such as natural disasters, strikes, or government actions.

Payment:

All fees and charges related to your shipments are due upon acceptance. We reserve the right to withhold delivery of your shipment until full payment is received.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website. We encourage you to review our privacy policy regularly to stay informed of any updates.

Contact Us:

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@sbatransportationinc.com
